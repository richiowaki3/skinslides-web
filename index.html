2026 Web Reconstruction
System Re-engineering & AI Logic: Richi Owaki, AI Agent
Conceived as an “interface for a permanent preservation of the dancer’s movements,” this video dance piece is based on the idea of producing images and sound first, and subsequently programming a data-choreography out of the single elements.

While the original 2009 version featured three 1.8m x 0.9m acrylic screens embedded in the floor, the 2026 Web update utilizes advanced dynamic video and acoustic analysis to newly analyze the 55 video clips and sound assets. This allows the physical dynamics of the footage to sync asynchronously and organically with Otomo Yoshihide's soundtrack, weaving a brand-new, infinitely generated choreography.
